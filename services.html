Step One - Technical Assessment

A brief assessment of your operations by one of our technical experts will identify development areas from the past two years and determine your eligibility for funding.

Step Two - Project Cost Assessment

A detailed review of project costs will be conducted to ensure all potential sources of SR&ED and IDMTC funding are claimed. Our team of technical specialists will work on assembling necessary technical summaries and associated documents.

3

Step Three - Review and Filing

Technical and financial documents will be broken down and reviewed with you to ensure their accuracy. This information is then tabulated and formatted to meet the guidelines of the SR&ED and IDMTC programs.

4

Step Four - Follow-up and Support

We are committed to ensuring your business receives the SR&ED funding it deserves and will help gather and provide any information requested during the approval process. We will also help you identify and track critical technical and financial metrics to maximize future claims.
