About SR&ED and IDMTC

The Scientific Research & Experimental Development (SR&ED) program is the largest funding initiative for businesses owned and operated in Canada. Every taxed company pays into this fund and is entitled to a refund for eligible work. Additional funding can be achieved through applicable Interactive Digital Media Tax Credit (IDMTC) provincial programs that may apply depending on the work.

Companies involved in developing new or improved products, processes, software development or associated systems can claim refunds for eligible hours worked, material expenses and contractor costs.

Time is the most important factor with SR&ED and IDMTC claims. Your filing deadline will be quickly approaching if you have worked on a new or ongoing project in the last two years.
